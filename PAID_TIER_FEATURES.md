# YouTube Outlier Finder — Paid Tier Feature Lists

## Free — Explorer

**Purpose:** Prove the signal is real.

- Search for outlier videos by keyword
- View up to 5 outliers per search
- Virality multiplier (views ÷ subscribers)
- Confidence tiers (Promising / Strong / Breakout)
- Plain-English "Why this is an outlier" explanation

**Free is intentionally limited.**
It shows what's possible, not a full workflow.

---

## Pro — Creator ($19–$29 / month)

**Purpose:** Turn discovery into repeatable wins.

*"Help me consistently find ideas that work for channels my size."*

### Core features

- Unlimited searches
- View all outliers for every search
- Subscriber cap filters (e.g. under 10k / 50k)
- View floor filters (remove low-signal noise)
- Sort by:
  - Virality multiplier
  - Recency
- Save searches (keyword / niche watches)

### Automation

- Weekly Personalized Outlier Feed
  - Automatically curated based on saved niches
  - Delivered by email
- Alerts when new outliers appear in saved niches (email)

### Why people pay for Pro

- No manual checking
- Ideas come to them
- Clear, repeatable content pipeline

**This tier alone justifies a subscription.**

---

## Growth — Serious Creator / Operator ($39–$59 / month)

**Purpose:** First-mover advantage + execution clarity.

*"Tell me what to make before it's obvious — and how to replicate it."*

**Everything in Pro, plus:**

### Velocity / First-Mover Alerts ⭐

Alerts when a video:

- crosses the outlier threshold
- AND is still accelerating (views/hour)

**Delivery:**

- Email (v1)
- Slack / Discord webhook (later)

### "Why It Worked" Blueprint Reports ⭐

One-click AI deconstruction:

- Hook analysis (first 30–60 seconds)
- Title structure breakdown
- Thumbnail comparison vs channel baseline
- Structural map (conflict → payoff → pattern interrupts)

**This turns:**

"Interesting video"
into
"I know exactly how to remake this."

### Emerging Meta Insights

- Weekly digest:
  - "5 emerging outlier patterns in your niche"
- Topic clustering:
  - Multiple small channels → same concept

### Why people upgrade to Growth

- Timing advantage
- Less guessing
- Faster execution
- Higher ROI per video

**This tier supports higher pricing because it saves time and mistakes.**

---

## Scale — Teams / Agencies (Later) ($99+ / month)

**Purpose:** Competitive intelligence + portfolio management.

*"Show me what my competitors are missing — before they dominate it."*

**Everything in Growth, plus:**

- Competitor channel tracking
- Gap analysis:
  - Alerts when competitors hit outliers outside their usual topics
- Multiple brand / channel profiles
- Advanced exports (Notion / Trello / internal tools)
- Collaboration features

**This is not needed for v1, but defines the ceiling.**

---

## Tier Philosophy (important)

- **Free:** Proof
- **Pro:** Consistency
- **Growth:** Speed + confidence
- **Scale:** Strategic advantage

**You are not selling "more data".**
**You are selling less uncertainty and better timing.**
